Vocabulary on Operating Systems:

  • Operating System: the fundamental program your computer runs that allows you to navigate around the computer and run programs. The most well-known operating systems in use are MacOXS and Windows.

  • UNIX: Essentially (though not precisely), the original operating system. Nearly all other operating systems are in some way based upon UNIX. The commands we use, like pwd and ls originated with UNIX and are called UNIX commands.
