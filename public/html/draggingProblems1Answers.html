Situation 5

The acceleration and net force are both equal to zero because the block is moving at a constant velocity.

Force Magnitude How did you determine the magnitude?
Gravity 19.6 N \( F_g = mg = 2.00 \cdot 9.8 = 19.6 \text{N} \)
Normal Force 19.6 N Must balance gravity because the block is not accelerating vertically.
Applied Force 16.0 N Given in the problem
Friction 16.0 N For net force = 0, must be equal to the applied force.

The coefficient of kinetic friction is equal to 0.816.
\( F_{fr} = \mu_K F_N \\ 16.0 = \mu_K \cdot 19.6 \\ \mu_K = 0.816 \)
